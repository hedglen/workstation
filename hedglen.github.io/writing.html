Writing

Essays & Pieces

Creative nonfiction, craft notes, and long-form work.

Most of what I write circles back to the same questions: What does it cost to carry certain things forward? How much of the past lives in the present without being invited? What does it mean to be honest on the page about things that resist easy language? I'm working through those questions in public. This is where the work lives.
